The Spirit and the Witness

Acts narrates the movement of the risen Jesus through Spirit-filled witnesses crossing boundaries and carrying the Word toward the nations.

Stage 1 — Individual Work

Individual Preparation

Bring your adaptive-learning work, write your own starting point, and use AI only as a questioning coach.

Stage 2 — Group Work

Group Discussion and Presentation

Compare answers, map tension, use AI as dialogue catalyst, and present a shared synthesis.

This app is for active learning, not passive answer production. Interpret first, let AI question, then discern together.
